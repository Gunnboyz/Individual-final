What are bosses?

Bosses are the main way of progressing in Terraria. Each Terraria boss offers unique and challenging attack patterns and situations that no two bosses feel the same. Bosses are by far the most difficult thing about Terraria and while beating every boss is not required to complete the game, each boss drops useful items that could help you later down the road. Preparing for each boss is the main gameplay loop that Terraria players cant get enough of. While challenging, every boss has their weak spot and exposing and really leaning into that bosses weakness is the key to victory! Ranging from the eye of a god to the lord of the moon Terraria offers many a variety of formitable foes.
